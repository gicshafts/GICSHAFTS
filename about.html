About Us

About Us

GOEL INDUSTRIAL CORPORATION is a fullcapability provider of high quality precision turned components, Wiper Motors Components such as wiper Shafts, Gear shaft, Shifting Shafts, Alternator Pulley, Screw, Rivets, pins, Bolts, nuts, Ball joints, Lock Components, CNC Components and other automotive parts solutions as per customer requirement for automobile applications, serving automobile manufacturers in India and abroad.

Mission

To retain and reinforce our position as a leading Indian Manufacturer of precision automotive parts, to meet the aspirations of customers in domestic and foreign markets. Thus, contributing to a better world by creating value together with a vision for the future.

Vision

We, the proud members of the GOEL INDUSTRIAL CORPORATION, shall strive vigorously to delight our customers who are our very purpose, by pursuing excellence and innovation through committed team work. To this end we shall promote continuous learning, achievement orientation and ethical business practices.

Management Principles

To be creative in thought and steady in action
To be cooperative and pioneering
To be trustworthy by improving ourselves
